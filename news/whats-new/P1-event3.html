Where does HKRS Rabbit go?

2023 What's New - 13 April 2023

HKRS hopes to increase transparency by publishing statistics from time to time, and also hopes to let the public pay attention to a group of abandoned rabbits who are old or sick.

According to our latest statistics on April 11, 2023, the following is the number of existing rabbits in HKRS:

  • 0~1 year old: 0
  • 2~3 years old: 26
  • 4~5 years old: 28
  • 6~9 years old: 24
  • 10 years old or above: 22

There are a total of 99 rabbits, and 61 of them are receiving medical treatment, that is, more than 60% (61.6%) of the total are sick abandoned rabbits. In other words, only 38 rabbits do not need follow-up medical treatment.
