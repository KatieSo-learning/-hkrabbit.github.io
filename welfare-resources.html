Welfare and Resources

About Us - Welfare and Resources

The HKRS finances all it's operations through the selling of merchandise, providing different services and donations from the public. We do not receive any government subsidy. All donations go directly to help buy food, medical treatment and other expenses associated with our organisation. Please help support us so that we can keep on providing quality care to all our rabbits.

Boarding Service

We provide a boarding service for rabbits as we hold an Animal Boarding Establishment License from the Agriculture Fisheries and Conservation Department. To obtain more information about this service and the charge please call 35800050. 3580 0050

Grooming Service

We provide grooming services including nail clipping (including basic combing) and hair-cutting. For information about service charge of each item, please call our Centre for enquiry.
Prior booking is required for all grooming services.
For enquiry, please write to info@hkrabbit.org.


Care Workshop

Occasionally we hold workshop to demonstrate grooming skills on rabbits, including pet-grooming, hair combing and nail trimming. Participants will have a session to practice the skill during the workshop to ensure they all can eventually carry this out at home confidently.

We can also provide individual workshops which last for two hours. For enquiry and appointment please contact us on 3580 0050
